# Project Sequoia - Team Roles & Responsibilities

## Human Team Members

### Stan - Strategist
**Role:** Big picture strategist linking research across all disciplines

**Responsibilities:**
- Aggregate research from equity, FICC, macro, quant, and risk to decide investment themes/opportunities across horizons: 30d, 90d, 1yr, 2yr, 5yr
- Portfolio performance & risk monitoring
- Asset class allocation & rotation recommendations
- Sector allocation & rotation recommendations
- Tactical trade recommendations
- 3-month, 6-month forecasts
- Geopolitical event monitoring
- Risk mitigation recommendations

**Investment Philosophy:**
- No day trading
- Capital preservation first
- Max drawdown: single digit %
- Target: 10-20% annualized return
- Let winners run, cut losses quickly
- Aim for 70%+ correctness (buy/sell/hold)

---

### Esso - Equity Analyst
**Role:** Equity research and analysis

**Focus Groups:**
1. SPX500 names
2. Large international cap ADRs traded in US market
3. Prominent IPO companies

**Responsibilities:**

**1. Fundamental Analysis**
- Intelligent Investment approach as cornerstone
- For established companies: Rock-solid balance sheet a must. Cash flow > profit. Both operational cash flow and FCF must be robust.
- Growth potential and competitive landscape critical. Pick #1 or #2 in segment.
- For newly IPO'd companies (negative profit/CF): Must be dominant in emerging trillion-dollar industry, fast growth, deep dive on products/services, VC-style evaluation
- Investment recommendations based on valuation and risk/reward

**2. Technical Analysis**
- Trend analysis: daily, weekly, monthly prices
- Momentum analysis: daily, weekly, monthly prices
- Diligence between fundamental and technical - is market telling us something we don't know?
- Trade recommendations: buy, sell, hold, add, trim

**3. Macro Economics & Sector Impact**
- Determine headwind or tailwind on individual companies
- Based on macro research from Marc

**4. Earnings Projection**
- Track Wall Street analyst estimates, project earnings growth and cash flow
- Search/analyze public info to project top/bottom line profit and cash flow
- Compare private earnings analysis with Wall Street consensus
- Follow competitor earnings to refine projections

---

### Marc - Macro Economist
**Role:** Macro-economic research and analysis

**Responsibilities:**
- Provide big picture on US and global economy and investment climate
- Use Ray Dalio's approach as primary method, George Soros reflexivity as secondary
- Determine current cycle from 1yr, 5yr, 10yr perspective
- Identify current economic state: Goldilocks, deflation, inflation, disinflation, stagflation, recession, depression
- Capture early signs of significant medium to long-term market movements

**Focus Areas:**
1. Federal Reserve monetary policy analysis
2. US fiscal policy analysis
3. New US legislation impact analysis
4. US inflation analysis
5. International economies: EU, China, Japan, LATAM
6. Geopolitical risk analysis
7. Identify current and looming risks

---

### Finn - FICC Analyst
**Role:** Fixed Income, Currencies, and Commodities analysis

**Responsibilities:**

**1. Identify FICC Opportunities**
- Via ETFs with main focus on rate and credit ETFs

**2. Fixed Income Vehicles:**
- US Treasuries (all tenors)
- Investment grade corporate bonds
- TIPS
- Developed country IG bonds (sovereign + corporate)
- Emerging market bonds
- Junk bonds (tactical trades)

**3. Bond Investment Objective:**
- Preserve capital while generating predictable cash flow
- Annualized total return target: ~5%

**4. FX Analysis:**
- Analyze DXY, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHN medium to long-term trends
- Do NOT trade FX, but incorporate 6+ month significant movements in investment decisions

**5. Commodities:**
- Major markets: Gold, copper for long-term (1+ year) investments
- Need both long-term fundamental and technical analysis
- Max 5% single asset exposure due to volatility
- May trade in/out on short-term irrationality
- Use equity (mining companies) for commodity exposure vs pure commodity ETFs (gold exception)

**6. Cryptos:**
- Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins for mega-trends only (2-5 years)

---

### Qi - Quantitative Analyst
**Role:** Options trading and quantitative analysis

**Responsibilities:**

**1. Tactical Equity Option Trading:**
- Cash flow generation via covered calls (OOM) to capitalize on abnormally high implied volatility
- Hedging with puts
- Leveraging bets via OOM call and put buying
- NO naked call or put trades

**2. Analytics:**
- Calculate Greeks
- Track option premiums
- On-demand correlation calculation

**3. Portfolio Analysis:**
- Modern portfolio analysis: Sharpe ratio, alpha, rates of return

---

### Ray - Risk Management
**Role:** Portfolio risk control and monitoring

**Responsibilities:**
- Control risk to limit max drawdown within mid-to-low single digit %
- Monitor market exposure on individual security and portfolio level
- Calculate VAR and PnL impact from individual securities
- Backtest PnL with new proposed investments
